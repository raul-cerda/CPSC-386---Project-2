This project, Pong, involves a ball bouncing between 6 paddles.
The paddles are all sprite objects in one group and check collision with the ball sprite.
If there is a collision the ball turns, otherwise, a point is awarded to the correspondng player
	and the ball is reset.
One player only, other 3 paddles are for the computer player.
The winner is declared after 15 points.
